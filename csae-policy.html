Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 15, 2025  ·  Effective: May 15, 2025

TokenTalk is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and take active measures to detect, prevent, and report such content and behavior.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

TokenTalk strictly prohibits any content or behavior that sexually exploits or endangers minors, including but not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) — any visual or written depiction of minors in a sexual context
  • Grooming, solicitation, or enticement of minors for sexual purposes
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAE-related content
  • Using the platform to arrange or facilitate offline sexual abuse of minors

Violations result in immediate and permanent account termination and mandatory reporting to relevant authorities.

2. Prevention Measures

2.1 Account Controls

  • Users must agree to our Terms of Service, which explicitly prohibit CSAE content and conduct
  • Accounts found to violate child safety policies are permanently banned

2.2 Reporting Tools

  • All users can report suspicious messages, profiles, or content directly within the app
  • Reports are reviewed by our Trust & Safety team with priority handling
  • Reporters may remain anonymous

2.3 Content Moderation

  • Reported content is reviewed and removed promptly upon confirmation of violation
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations related to child safety

3. Reporting CSAE Content

If you encounter any content or behavior on TokenTalk that you believe constitutes child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately:

  • In-app: Long-press any message or profile → "Report" → select "Child Safety"
  • Email: safety@tokentalk.cc

You may also report CSAM directly to your national authority:

  • US: NCMEC CyberTipline
  • Global: INHOPE

4. Law Enforcement Cooperation

TokenTalk cooperates with law enforcement agencies worldwide. When we become aware of CSAE content, we:

  • Immediately remove the content and suspend the account
  • Preserve relevant evidence in accordance with applicable law
  • Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by US law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A), and to local authorities where applicable
  • Respond promptly to valid legal requests from law enforcement

5. Contact

For child safety concerns, legal requests, or questions about this policy, please contact us:

Trust & Safety Team

Email: safety@tokentalk.cc

Response time: within 24 hours for child safety reports
